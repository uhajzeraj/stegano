What we offer




STEGANOGRAPHY

Steganography is the hiding of a secret message within an ordinary message and the extraction of it at its destination.


CAESAR's CIPHER

It is a type of substitution cipher in which each letter in the plaintext is replaced by a letter some fixed number of positions down the alphabet.


ROT 13

ROT13 is a simple letter substitution cipher that replaces a letter with the 13th letter after it, in the alphabet.
